Project Apex

High Altitude Photography Experiments

The Apex Project now has a new home at apexhab.org - however most content will also appear here.
The Apex Project was started with the aim of using high altitude meteorological balloons to take photographs and collect sensor data from near-space. Details of each individual payload is given on the relevant pages in this section.

Apex I: Built September 08 - April 09
  • Launched 25/04/09. (Lost payload!) (KML track)
Apex II: Built June 09 - July 10
  • Launched 17/07/10. Recovered. (KML track/Google Maps) (with photos)
  • Launched 09/04/11. Recovered. (KML track/Google Maps)
